\# Ambrook Brand Context \& Email Guidelines



\## 1. Core Identity \& Mission

\*\*Mission:\*\* The modern, mobile-first accounting platform built for the complex operations "big box" software ignores (QuickBooks is for retail; Ambrook is for the field).

\*\*The Goal:\*\* Move operators from "gut feel" to "data-driven" management and patch the "leaky ships" in their financial operations.



\## 2. Voice \& Tone Specs

\* \*\*Practical \& "In the Dirt":\*\* We speak the language of the truck, the barn, and the pasture—not the boardroom.

\* \*\*Partner, Not Vendor:\*\* Customers feel like Ambrook is a partner in their specific industry (e.g., the beef industry).

\* \*\*High-Value/Low-Effort:\*\* Focus on time recovered (e.g., "7 days of work down to 1 hour") and "peace of mind".

\* \*\*Confident \& US-Based:\*\* Emphasize 1:1 human support from experts who actually understand agriculture and trade.



\## 3. The "Anti-QuickBooks" Logic (Use for Hooks)

\* \*\*QuickBooks:\*\* Built for accountants/retail; charges extra for "classes"; requires desk time.

\* \*\*Ambrook:\*\* Built for operators; enterprise tracking (Schedule F/C) is native; work happens on mobile.



\## 4. Industry-Specific Terminologies

\* \*\*Farming:\*\* Production insights, Schedule F alignment, enterprise profitability (e.g., corn vs. soy).

\* \*\*Ranching:\*\* Managerial accounting in the field, tagging feed/vet costs to stocker enterprises while in the pasture.

\* \*\*Equestrian:\*\* Per-horse P\&Ls, breeding mare/stallion ROI, streamlined reimbursables.

\* \*\*Trucking:\*\* Cost-per-mile tracking, route profitability, "hauling blind" vs. "road-ready".

\* \*\*Construction:\*\* Project-level P\&Ls, vendor management, "back office in your pocket".



\## 5. Email Writing Constraints (CLI Strategy)

\* \*\*Mobile-First Formatting:\*\* Short, punchy sentences. Operators read these on phones at the pump, in the cab, or between chores.

\* \*\*Lead with "The Patch":\*\* Identify the "leak" (manual spreadsheets, lost receipts, audit anxiety) and show the immediate fix.

\* \*\*No Jargon:\*\* Swap "Managerial Accounting" for "Knowing why you made money." Swap "Compliance" for "Keeping the CPA happy".
