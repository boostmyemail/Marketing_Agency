\# Ambrook Tone Audit Script

Scan the provided email draft against the following criteria:



1\. \*\*Operational Focus:\*\* Is this focused on \*profitability\* (making money) or just \*compliance\* (tax prep)?

2\. \*\*Industry Accuracy:\*\* Use sector-specific terms like "Schedule F," "enterprise tracking," or "cost-per-mile."

3\. \*\*Device Check:\*\* Is the formatting optimized for a mobile screen (short paragraphs, clear headings)?

4\. \*\*Differentiator Check:\*\* Mention US-based human support or AI-powered scanning where relevant.
