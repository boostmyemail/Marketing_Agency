\# Glide Tone Audit Script

Scan the provided email draft against the following criteria:



1\. \*\*Banned Word Check:\*\* Flag any use of: \*next-level, cutting-edge, seamless, effortless, unlock, unleash, streamline, guaranteed, with one click, super-powered, all-in-one, game-changing, revolutionary\*.

2\. \*\*Punctuation Check:\*\* - Flag any em dashes (—) and suggest replacement with commas or periods.

&nbsp;  - Verify zero exclamation marks in the subject line.

&nbsp;  - Verify no more than one exclamation mark in the body.

3\. \*\*Voice Check:\*\* Is it confident and human, or does it sound like a "slogan"?

4\. \*\*Structural Check:\*\* Remove any "Imagine..." openers or "In a nutshell" summaries.
