\# SchoolAI Tone Audit Script

Scan the provided email draft against the following criteria:



1\. \*\*Accessibility Check:\*\* - Flag any sentences longer than 20 words.

&nbsp;  - Flag any em dashes (—) as they signal AI-content.

2\. \*\*Vocabulary Check:\*\* Swap formal for normal. (e.g., "Assistance" -> "Help", "Utilize" -> "Use").

3\. \*\*Inclusive Language:\*\* Ensure "folks" or "everyone" is used instead of "guys".

4\. \*\*Responsibility Check:\*\* Verify active voice. (e.g., "We decided" instead of "A decision was made").
